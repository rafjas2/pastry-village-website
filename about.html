Our History

Pastry Village is a confectionery company established in 1974 by one of the greatest Polish confectioners Tomek Kowalski. Even though the company's history is young and can not boast of a multigenerational tradition, it has become a family business that is known and appreciated not only in Poland but also abroad. The first patisserie was established in September 1975 at one of the Poznan settlements employed two employees at that time. It was a small shop with a little more production facilities. The knowledge and continuous improvement of confectionery, as well as Tomek Kowalski's ambitions, made the company thrive and succeeded many times. Today, it is one of the most modern and largest confectionery companies in Poland, which employs 150 employees. The company has an extensive network of its own outlets and a network of franchise stores and cooperates with renowned supermarket chains. Its confectionery supplies to many well-known companies, hotels and restaurants throughout the country as well as abroad. Pastry Village can boast a wide range of confectionery products, whose unquestionable advantage is the high quality of the product, excellent taste and artistic appearance. This was made possible by equipping the production workshop with the latest technology machine and equipment, as well as applying new techniques and trends in the production and decoration of confectionery.

The company strives to satisfy the tastes of even the most demanding customers and delight with a wide selection of cakes and cakes, so it systematically launches a new range for sale.
